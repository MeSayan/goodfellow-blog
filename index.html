A message from the Author

Hello and welcome to this blog.

This blog has been developed as part of my summer challenge. It is the culmination of three of my interests—reading, writing and web. Once I finish reading a book, I will put up an article about it. This is also part of my challenge.

The articles I put up, are mostly musings of my mind that I plan to share here.

A wise woman once said:

Your tale, sir, would cure deafness.
Miranda, Tempest, Shakespeare.

So buckle up please, this is my tale and I hope to cure my silence.

I will write mostly about books that I have read, and how they have impacted me.

I firmly believe that books are food for our brains. And if you are a book lover too, I hope this site will appeal to you.
